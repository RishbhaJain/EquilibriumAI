AMAZON LOGISTICS / FEDEX / UPS
LAST-MILE DELIVERY EMISSIONS — OWALA DIRECT-TO-CONSUMER & E-COMMERCE

================================================================================
This document consolidates last-mile delivery data for Owala FreeSip bottles
sold through e-commerce channels (Amazon, owala.com, Target.com, REI.com)
during Q3-Q4 2025.
================================================================================

CHANNEL BREAKDOWN — E-COMMERCE UNITS SHIPPED (Q3-Q4 2025):

Channel              | Units  | Avg Package Wt | Avg Delivery Distance
---------------------|--------|----------------|----------------------

Owala.com (DTC)      |  3,200 | 0.68 kg        | 620 miles (avg ship dist)
Target.com (ship)    |  2,100 | 0.65 kg        | 185 miles (from store/DC)

================================================================================
LAST-MILE EMISSION FACTORS
================================================================================

Amazon Logistics (AMZL):
  Fleet mix (2024): 65% diesel vans, 20% Rivian EV, 15% flex/contractor
  Avg emissions per package (US, 2024): 0.408 kg CO2e
  Source: Amazon 2024 Sustainability Report

  For Owala packages (heavier than average, 0.62 kg vs 0.35 kg avg):
  Weight-adjusted factor: ~0.52 kg CO2e per package

FedEx Ground (Owala.com DTC shipments):
  Avg emissions per package (US):  0.68 kg CO2e (ground, avg distance)
  For avg 620-mile shipment:       1.12 kg CO2e per package
  Source: FedEx 2024 ESG Report, SmartWay data

UPS Ground (some Owala.com shipments):
  Avg emissions per package:       0.72 kg CO2e
  For avg 500-mile shipment:       1.05 kg CO2e per package
  Source: UPS 2024 ESG Report

Target Shipt / last-mile:
  Store-to-door: 0.45 kg CO2e (short distance, pooled delivery)
  DC-to-door:    0.85 kg CO2e (regional)

REI Ship-from-DC:
  Avg: 0.92 kg CO2e per package

================================================================================
ESTIMATED LAST-MILE EMISSIONS (Q3-Q4 2025)
================================================================================

Amazon FBA:      12,400 × 0.52  =  6,448 kg CO2e
Owala.com DTC:    3,200 × 1.12  =  3,584 kg CO2e
Target.com:       2,100 × 0.85  =  1,785 kg CO2e
REI.com:            800 × 0.92  =    736 kg CO2e

TOTAL LAST-MILE EMISSIONS:        12,553 kg CO2e (12.6 tonnes)

Per unit (e-commerce only):        0.678 kg CO2e per bottle

PACKAGING FOR E-COMMERCE (additional)
================================================================================

E-commerce orders require additional outer shipping packaging:

  Amazon FBA: Poly mailer or corrugated shipper box
    Weight: ~85g per package
    Material: Corrugated or recycled poly
    Emissions from packaging production: ~0.05 kg CO2e per package

  DTC (Owala.com): Branded corrugated shipper
    Weight: ~120g
    Emissions: ~0.07 kg CO2e per package

  Total e-commerce packaging emissions: ~1,050 kg CO2e
