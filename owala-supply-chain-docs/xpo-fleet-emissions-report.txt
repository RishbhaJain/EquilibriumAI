================================================================================
DIESEL VEHICLE EMISSION FACTORS
================================================================================

Class 8 Drayage Truck (typical port-to-warehouse run):

  Loaded (with container, ~20,000-44,000 lbs gross):
    Fuel consumption:     5.0 — 6.5 MPG (varies by weight, traffic, route)
    Fleet average:        5.5 MPG loaded
    CO2 per mile:         1.845 kg CO2 / mile
                          (= 10.15 kg/gal ÷ 5.5 MPG)
    Per km:               1.146 kg CO2 / km

  Empty (returning chassis, no container):
    Fuel consumption:     6.5 — 8.0 MPG
    Fleet average:        7.0 MPG empty
    CO2 per mile:         1.450 kg CO2 / mile
    Per km:               0.901 kg CO2 / km

  Round trip factor (loaded + empty return):
    Average per loaded-mile: 3.295 kg CO2 / loaded-mile
    (assumes equal distance loaded and empty)

Long Beach → Ontario DC route:
  Distance:               55 miles (one way), 110 miles round trip
  Loaded trip CO2:        55 × 1.845 = 101.5 kg CO2
  Empty return CO2:       55 × 1.450 = 79.8 kg CO2
  ROUND TRIP TOTAL:       181.3 kg CO2 per container move
