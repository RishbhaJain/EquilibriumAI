MARINE FUEL PRODUCTS — EMISSION FACTORS
================================================================================

1. VERY LOW SULPHUR FUEL OIL (VLSFO)
   Specification:      ISO 8217:2024, RMG 380 with ≤0.50% sulphur
   Density:            0.96 — 0.99 kg/liter (at 15°C)
   Energy Content:     40.2 MJ/kg (LHV)
   Carbon Content:     ~86.5%

   EMISSION FACTORS:
     Tank-to-Wake (combustion only):
       CO2:            3.114 kg CO2 / kg fuel
       CH4:            0.006 kg CO2e / kg fuel
       N2O:            0.002 kg CO2e / kg fuel
       TOTAL TtW:      3.122 kg CO2e / kg fuel

     Well-to-Tank (upstream production & delivery):
       Extraction:     0.18 kg CO2e / kg fuel
       Refining:       0.12 kg CO2e / kg fuel
       Transport:      0.05 kg CO2e / kg fuel
       TOTAL WtT:      0.35 kg CO2e / kg fuel

     WELL-TO-WAKE (TOTAL LIFECYCLE):
       TOTAL WtW:      3.472 kg CO2e / kg fuel
       Per MJ:         86.4 g CO2e / MJ

ROAD TRANSPORT FUELS — EMISSION FACTORS
================================================================================

CHEVRON CORPORATION — FUEL CARBON DATA (US MARKET)
(Source: Chevron 2024 Climate Change Resilience Report)

1. ULTRA-LOW SULFUR DIESEL (ULSD) — US road transport
   Density:            0.832 kg/liter (at 15°C)
   Energy Content:     35.86 MJ/liter | 43.1 MJ/kg
   Carbon Content:     ~86.2%

   Tank-to-Wheel:      2.68 kg CO2 / liter
                        = 10.15 kg CO2 / US gallon
                        = 73.15 g CO2 / MJ
   Well-to-Wheel:      3.24 kg CO2e / liter (incl. upstream)

2. REGULAR GASOLINE (E10, 10% ethanol blend)
   Density:            0.745 kg/liter
   Tank-to-Wheel:      2.31 kg CO2 / liter
                        = 8.74 kg CO2 / US gallon
   Well-to-Wheel:      2.92 kg CO2e / liter

3. B20 BIODIESEL BLEND (20% biodiesel, 80% ULSD)
   Tank-to-Wheel:      2.46 kg CO2 / liter (biogenic CO2 excluded)
   Well-to-Wheel:      2.80 kg CO2e / liter
   Lifecycle reduction: ~14% vs pure ULSD

4. COMPRESSED NATURAL GAS (CNG) — heavy-duty vehicles
   Tank-to-Wheel:      2.75 kg CO2 / kg CNG
   Well-to-Wheel:      3.20 kg CO2e / kg CNG
   Equivalent:         ~1.89 kg CO2e / diesel-liter-equivalent

NOTES & METHODOLOGY
================================================================================

  - Marine fuel emission factors per IMO Fourth GHG Study (2020) & IMO
    Revised Guidelines (MEPC.364(79))
  - Road fuel factors per US EPA Emission Factors Hub (2024) and
    IPCC 2006 Guidelines (2019 refinement)
  - Well-to-tank factors per JEC Well-to-Wheels Analysis v5
  - Grid factors per IEA CO2 Emissions from Fuel Combustion (2024)
    and China MEE Regional Grid Emission Factors (2024 update)

  These factors are provided for GHG Protocol Scope 3 accounting purposes.
  Actual emissions may vary based on engine efficiency, load factors,
  ambient conditions, and fuel batch composition.
